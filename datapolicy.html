Data Collection Policy

Main website

We do not collect any data beyond what you yourself submit via forms or by sending an email to addresses displayed on the website.

The data we receive will be used as follows:

  • The Online Statement Form is broken on purpose. You can enter anything you want into the fields; clicking "Submit" will not send any data to our servers at all.
  • The Guest Book form will collect the data you submit and use it to automatically create a new post on the Guest Book page. If you submitted something and would like to have it removed, please contact us at magnuswebsiteproject@gmail.com.
  • The Contact form will [reword/make sure that's how it works: collect your message and email address so our autoresponder email can send you an automated message about it]. We do not keep your email address beyond that and we will not use it for any other purposes.
  • If you send an email to mail@themagnusinstitute.org or tech@themagnusinstitute.org, an automatic response will be sent to your email address. We do not keep your email address beyond that and we will not use it for any other purposes.
